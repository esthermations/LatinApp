===Alternative forms===
* {{l|la|pulcer}}

===Etymology===
{{unknown|la}}. Possibly from earlier {{m|la|polcher}}, which according to Walde-Hoffman and Pokorny reflects {{der|la|ine-pro|*perḱ-||motley, variegated}}, with dissimilation {{m|ine-pro||*perḱ-ro-s}} > {{m|ine-pro||*pelḱ-ro-s}}, which would make it cognate to {{cog|sa|पृश्नि|tr=pṛ́śni}}. De Vaan<ref>{{R:itc:EDL|page=496}}</ref> rejects that connection as both irregular and semantically incompatible/tenuous, and assigns no known etymology.
