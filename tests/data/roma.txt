==Latin==

===Etymology===
There are some theories:
* Roman mythology derived the name from {{m|la|Rōmulus}}, name of the legendary founder and first king.

* The word may derive from "Rumon" or "Rumen", an archaic name for the [[Tiber]] river related to the Indo-European root {{der|la|ine-pro|*srew-||to flow}}.

* The word may be of {{bor|la|ett|-}} origin, as {{m|ett|𐌓𐌖𐌌𐌀}} was one of the Etruscan {{m|la|gens|gentes}}, from {{m|ett|*𐌓𐌖𐌌||teat}}.

* From {{bor|la|grc|-}} word {{m|grc|ῥόμη|lit=strength}}; see {{m|grc|ῥώννυμι}}.
