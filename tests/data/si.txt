==Latin==

===Alternative forms===
* {{alter|la|sei||standard in Republican spelling}}

===Etymology===
From {{inh|la|itc-pro|*sei||so, thus}} used in parataxis, likely via the meaning "in this" as the locative singular of {{der|la|ine-pro|*só||this, that}}; this older meaning is preserved in {{cog|la|sīc}} as well as in the oath {{m|la|sī dīs placet}}, c.f. English ''so help me God''. Related to {{m+|ang|sē||he, that}}.
